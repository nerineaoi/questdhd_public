# QuestDHD Overview

**QuestDHD** turns ADHD management into an interactive, game-like experience.

## Concept
Players (users) complete "quests" representing daily tasks, earn rewards, and monitor their mental energy cycles.

## Vision
Create a system that motivates through **curiosity, feedback, and progression**, not guilt or pressure.

## Status
- Concept & design: in progress
- MVP: planned for private repository
- Focus: gamification + ADHD management + gentle structure
